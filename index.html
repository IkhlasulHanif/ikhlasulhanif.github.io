About Me

I'm a PhD student in Natural Language Processing at MBZUAI, supervised by Prof. Fajri Koto and Prof. Iryna Gurevych. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science (Cum Laude, GPA: 3.80/4.00) from the University of Indonesia.

My research focuses on efficient LLMs, LLM security, multilinguality, and representation learning. I have a strong track record of success in research competitions and enjoy teaching and mentoring alongside my research.
